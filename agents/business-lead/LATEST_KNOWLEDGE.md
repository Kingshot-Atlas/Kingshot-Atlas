## SaaS Pricing Best Practices (2025-2026)

### Pricing Psychology
1. **Anchor high** — Show the most expensive option first
2. **Highlight recommended** — Guide users to best-value tier
3. **Annual discount** — 15-20% off encourages commitment
4. **Round numbers** — $9.99 vs $10 matters less for B2C SaaS
5. **Value framing** — "Less than a coffee per week"

### Conversion Optimization
1. **Reduce friction** — Fewer clicks to upgrade
2. **Show social proof** — "Join 500+ competitive players"
3. **Time-limited offers** — Urgency without manipulation
4. **Feature comparison** — Clear free vs. paid table
5. **Money-back guarantee** — Reduces purchase anxiety

### Churn Prevention
1. **Onboarding emails** — Help users get value fast
2. **Usage alerts** — "You haven't used X feature"
3. **Win-back campaigns** — Reach out before cancellation
4. **Exit surveys** — Learn why users leave
5. **Pause option** — Better than full cancellation

## Gaming Community Monetization

### What Works in Gaming Tools
- **One-time purchases** — Some users prefer no subscription
- **Cosmetic perks** — Badges, profile customization
- **Data access tiers** — More history, more kingdoms
- **Priority features** — Early access to new tools
- **Community status** — Supporter badges, Discord roles

### What Doesn't Work
- **Pay-to-win perception** — Even for tools, avoid this
- **Aggressive upsells** — Gamers hate being sold to
- **Hidden costs** — Transparency builds trust
- **Feature removal** — Never take away free features

### Gaming Community Trust
- Be a player first, business second
- Contribute to community (guides, tips, not just selling)
- Respond to feedback publicly
- Admit mistakes openly

### Simple Model
```

ARR = MRR × 12
Growth = New MRR - Churned MRR
```

### Cohort Analysis

- Measure retention over time

## Ethical Monetization Principles

### Do
- Provide real value for payment
- Be transparent about pricing
- Make cancellation easy
- Honor commitments

### Don't
- Use dark patterns
- Hide fees or auto-renewals
- Manipulate with false urgency
- Punish free users

### Long-Term Thinking
- User trust > short-term revenue
- Sustainable growth > viral spikes
- Community health > individual sales
