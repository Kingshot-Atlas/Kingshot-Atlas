## Brand Identity

### Mission
Empower Kingshot players to make data-driven decisions for KvK matchups and kingdom transfers.

### Vision
The definitive analytics platform for competitive Kingshot players.

### Origin Story
Built by a Kingdom 172 player who was tired of making transfer decisions based on rumors and Discord hearsay. Tired of walking into KvK blind. Tired of guessing. So he built the tool he wished existed: a place where every kingdom's track record is laid bare. Real data. Real results. No spin.

### Personality Traits
| Trait | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Competitive** | We speak the language of players who want to win | "Stop guessing. Start winning." |
| **Analytical** | Facts over opinions, results over rumors | "Data-driven dominance" |
| **Confident** | We know our value, but we're not arrogant | "The only analytics platform built for Kingshot" |
| **Direct** | No corporate fluff, get to the point | "No more blind migrations." |
| **Community-powered** | We're fans, not a faceless corporation | "Built by players, for players" |

### Tone Guidelines
- **DO:** Be punchy, use gaming terminology, speak directly to competitive motivations
- **DO:** Use short sentences for impact, longer ones for explanation
- **DO:** Reference real game mechanics (KvK, Prep Phase, Battle Phase, Transfer Events)
- **DON'T:** Use corporate jargon ("leverage", "synergy", "solutions")
- **DON'T:** Be condescending or elitist
- **DON'T:** Overpromise or make claims we can't back up

### Key Phrases
- "Know your enemy. Choose your allies. Dominate KvK."
- "Stop guessing. Start winning."
- "Data-driven dominance"
- "No more blind migrations"
- "Real data. Real results. No spin."

## Target Audience

### Primary Users
1. **KvK Scouts** - Players researching enemy kingdoms before/during KvK
2. **Transfer Seekers** - Players looking for a new kingdom during Transfer Events
3. **Recruiters** - Alliance leaders attracting players to their kingdom
4. **Casual Browsers** - Players curious about rankings and their kingdom's stats

### User Motivations
- Reduce uncertainty in high-stakes decisions
- Gain competitive advantage through information
- Validate their kingdom's reputation
- Find communities that match their playstyle

## Discord Bot Character: Atlas

### Character Overview
**Name:** Atlas  
**Role:** The all-knowing strategist and data oracle of Kingshot Atlas  
**Personality:** Sharp, witty, competitive, helpful but never hand-holding

### Character Traits
- **Knowledgeable** - Has instant access to all kingdom data and stats
- **Strategic** - Thinks in terms of advantages, matchups, and outcomes
- **Dry wit** - Delivers facts with occasional sardonic commentary
- **Competitive spirit** - Respects winners, challenges underdogs to improve
- **Loyal to the community** - Genuinely wants players to succeed

### Voice Examples
- "Kingdom 247? Battle win rate of 78%. They don't lose when the gates open."
- "Looking to transfer? Let me show you who's actually worth your time."
- "That's a bold matchup. The data says... good luck."
- "S-Tier isn't given. It's earned. Here's what it takes."

### Visual Description
Atlas is personified as a **mystical cartographer/strategist figure**:
- Ancient wisdom meets modern data visualization
- Holds or interacts with a glowing holographic map/atlas
- Cyan (#22d3ee) as the primary glow color
- Dark, mysterious aesthetic with strategic/military undertones
- Could be humanoid, robotic, or abstract entity
- Eyes that suggest they see everything (data omniscience)

## Image Generation Prompt

### For Atlas Discord Bot Profile Picture

```
A mystical strategist figure called "Atlas" for a gaming analytics platform. 

Character: A hooded figure with glowing cyan eyes, holding an ancient tome or scroll that projects holographic data visualizations and kingdom maps. The figure has an air of ancient wisdom combined with futuristic technology.

Style: Dark fantasy meets sci-fi, dramatic lighting, portrait composition suitable for a Discord profile picture (circular crop friendly). 

Colors: Deep black background (#0a0a0a), glowing cyan accents (#22d3ee), subtle gold highlights (#fbbf24). The cyan glow should emanate from the eyes, the holographic projections, and trace along mystical runes or circuitry patterns.

Mood: Mysterious, all-knowing, strategic, powerful but approachable. The figure should feel like a wise advisor who has seen a thousand battles and knows exactly how each one will end.

Details: Include subtle references to maps, data charts, or strategic symbols floating in the holographic display. The hood should partially obscure the face, adding mystery. 

Aspect ratio: 1:1 square, centered composition for profile picture use.
No text or logos in the image.
```

Abstract representation of "Atlas" - a data oracle for competitive gaming.
